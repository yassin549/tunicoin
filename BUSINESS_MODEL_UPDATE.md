## ‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL LEGAL REQUIREMENTS**

### **Before Launch, You MUST:**

#### 1. **Securities Registration**
- Register as an Investment Advisor with SEC (US) or equivalent
- File Form ADV and obtain RIA license
- Or partner with registered broker-dealer
- Compliance with Investment Advisers Act of 1940

#### 2. **Money Services Business (MSB)**
- Register with FinCEN
- Obtain state-by-state MSB licenses
- Implement AML/KYC procedures
- File Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs)

#### 3. **Legal Disclosures**
- **Cannot promise guaranteed returns** without proper structure
- Risk disclaimers on every page
- Past performance disclaimers
- "Investments may lose value" warnings
- Detailed fee schedules
- Conflict of interest disclosures

#### 4. **Custody & Escrow**
- Cannot commingle user funds with personal funds
- Must use qualified custodian
- Regular audits required
- Insurance for user deposits

#### 5. **Tax Compliance**
- Issue 1099-MISC or 1099-B to users
- Report income to IRS
- Withholding for non-US users
- State tax compliance

#### 6. **Operational Requirements**
- Business entity formation (LLC, C-Corp)
- Business bank accounts (separate from personal)
- Accounting system for investor funds
- Legal counsel on retainer
- Compliance officer
- Insurance (E&O, D&O, cyber liability)

## üö® **REGULATORY RISKS**

### Potential Legal Issues

#### **Securities Fraud:**
- Offering unregistered securities
- Making false/misleading statements about returns
- Failure to disclose risks

**Penalty:** Up to $5M fine + 20 years prison (per violation)

#### **Ponzi Scheme:**
- Using new deposits to pay old investors
- Unsustainable return promises
- Lack of actual trading activity

**Penalty:** Criminal fraud charges, asset seizure, restitution

#### **Money Laundering:**
- Accepting deposits without KYC
- No AML monitoring
- Facilitating illegal transactions

**Penalty:** Up to $500K fine + 20 years prison

#### **Wire Fraud:**
- Using internet/phone to defraud investors
- False representations about investment

**Penalty:** Up to 20 years prison per count

- Operate in crypto-friendly jurisdiction (Cayman, Singapore, Switzerland)
- Accept only crypto deposits
- Market as crypto hedge fund
- Still requires local licensing
